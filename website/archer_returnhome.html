The End

Ingrid decided to return home to her father with her riches, she was also promised land back in her home country which she could move her father to and build a settlement of her own, she already knew some of the men in the crew would follow her when they returned home.

When she returned home, she travlled straight to her father with her cart full of riches, the town people couldn't believe their eyes when she returned to the town, she had done it, she had actually done it. Ingrid had people that had spat on her before come to her asking for money and to live with her on her new land, she refused and told them to leave her be. She quickly took her father and all their things and travelled to their land and started a settlement which quickly people learned of across the land.

Ingrid built the settlement up to be a trading hub for across the land to ensure that she would be rich for the rst of her days. Eventually she found a husband whome she shared all her riches with, he brought knowledge and respect with him and alliances with other tribes from across the land.
