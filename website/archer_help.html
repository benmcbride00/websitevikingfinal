Chapter 3

Ingrid decided to help the captain. She unsheathed her sword and swipped at the tenticle holding the captain before it could pull him away, cutting it off from the rest of her body. Her next move was to qucikly draw her bow and shot an arrow into the head of the beast, then when it turned to attack Ingrid she dropped her bow and pulled her sword out stabbing the creating in the head before it had a chance to attack her killing it before it could do any more damage. The rest of the crew watched her in shock, none of them could believe what had just happened.

"Thank you, you saved my life" said the captain.

"No worries, I had to help." laughed Ingrid

The captain promoted Ingrid to his right hand and rewarded her with a new sword which he had brought with him as extra, the sword was the same size as the one she had but was beautifully crafted.

The days went on when they finally arrived in England, they decided to go in land to a village which they captured and destroyed taking all the riches they could from this land, before they returned home, Ingrid had a decision to make. Does she go home as a hero with the riches she has just got and the promise of land from the captain, whom she saved the life off, or does she stay in this ladn with her riches and try to make a new life for herself
