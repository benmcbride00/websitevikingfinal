The End

Ingrid decided to stay in England with a couple of other men from the crew. The rest returned home. Ingrid and the men that stayed decided to travel around England in search of a place to settle.

Ingrid found a small village down the south of England where she met a man who she fell in love with and decided to buy land with and build their own settlement with a farm and other things on it. Eventually the small settlement grew into a small town which she ran like a Nordic town as the jarl of the town. Quickly people hear about the town and came from all over to live in it.

Ingrid's town became a hub for trading in the area and had some of the mightiest warriors in the land. Ingrid still fought alongside her men and women, her might was talked about across the land, everyone respected Ingrid and she became the best archer the land had seen. Word of her reached her father back in Trondheim who decided to travel to her town to see her again. Many Nordic men travlled from their home land to see and live in the town she had created.
